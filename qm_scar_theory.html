Quantum Mechanics Scar Theory

This project explores Quantum Chaos and Quantum Scar phenomena.

Objectives

  • Study quantum scars in chaotic systems.
  • Analyze wavefunction localization along classical periodic orbits.
  • Visualize quantum-classical correspondence.

Tools Used

Python, Matplotlib, NumPy

Results

The project shows how quantum wavefunctions localize along classical unstable orbits, demonstrating scar effects in quantum systems.
